Ponniyin Selvan 1 & 2

Ponniyin Selvan is a historical fiction novel series
by Kalki Krishnamurthy, which tells the story of
Arulmozhivarman (later known as Raja Raja Chola I) and
the Chola dynasty in the 10th and 11th centuries.
